GRADUATION DAY EVENT

The Graduation Day session at SPCOM 2018 will highlight the research of PhD students close to graduation. We received several nominations and selected the following candidates from universities across India and other participating institutions. Out of these shortlisted candidates, two talks will be chosen as the Best Graduation Day Session talks and awarded a certificate and a gift voucher from Springer. Please join us and get a 15-minute summary of these outstanding research efforts.
