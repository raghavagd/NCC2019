NATIONAL CONFERENCE ON COMMUNICATIONS (NCC): SILVER JUBILEE EDITION

The twenty-fifth National Conference on Communications (NCC 2019) will be held at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, during 20-23 February 2019. The conference will feature three symposia in the following areas.

COMMUNICATIONS NETWORKS SIGNAL PROCESSING

Previously unpublished contributions from a broad range of topics in these areas are solicited, including (but not limited to) the following:

Communication theory and systems Next generation networks Signal processing for communications
Information theory Optical networks and systems Image and video signal processing
Coding theory Sensor networks Multimedia applications
Wireless communications Ad hoc networks DSP algorithms and architectures
Optical communications Wireless and wireline networks Speech and audio processing
RF and microwave communications Security Biomedical applications
Satellite communications Networks and services management Language identification
Cognitive radio Performance analysis Machine learning, pattern analysis, and classification
Energy harvesting and green communications Complex networks Large dimensional signal processing
Machine learning for communications Biological networks Sparse signal processing
5G and beyond communication systems Cyber-physical systems/IoT Graph signal processing
Quantum communications

Researchers working in emerging fields of these areas are encouraged to submit their original findings. Submitted papers should be 5 pages in length in standard IEEE conference format and will be peer-reviewed by experts in the field. Detailed information on paper submission, technical program, accommodation, tutorials, and travel will be posted on the conference website: http://ece.iisc.ac.in/~ncc2019/.

First call for papers can be downloaded from here.
