REGISTRATION


Registration categories and charges

A single conference registration includes the following:

  • Access to all SPCOM conference sessions (July 17-19)
  • Conference proceedings
  • Refreshments and lunch (July 17-19)
  • Conference dinner
  • Conference kit containing bag and stationery items

There are two types of conference registration categories - full registration and student registration. Please note the following conditions before registering.

  • Each accepted paper needs at least one author to have a full registration by May 30, 2018. So for an accepted paper, if the only author registering for SPCOM is a student then he/she will have to register at full rate.
  • For authors, one full registration covers for uploading three accepted papers.
  • The final manuscript submission deadline is Wednesday, May 09, 2018, and the program will be finalized immediately after this date. One author's full registration will be checked before including each paper in the program (and the proceedings).
  • The early registration (early bird) discount applies only till May 23, 2018.

Tutorial registration

SPCOM 2018 will feature six tutorials on July 16. A single tutorial registration includes all the tutorials on July 16, and you can attend all the tutorials of your choice for the entire day.

Please note that the conference registration (student or regular/full) does not include the tutorials; they have to be registered for separately.


On-site registration: On-site registrations will be available on the days of the conference — July 16-19 — starting from 8 am each day at the conference venue. On-site registrations will also be availabe for the tutorials, on July 16.

*Note: "One day only" registration cannot be done online. Payment must be made in cash. The conference kit will not be provided to "One day only" registrants.

Each paper requires a "regular" registration for "Conference" by an author. One "regular" registration for "Conference" covers up to three papers by the same author. (Example: If you are a student, and you are the only author who is going to register, then you must choose "regular" registration for conference. You can register as a student for the tutorials).

You must register and pay before you upload your final manuscript. If you are paying by DD, please note that you can upload your final manuscript only after we have acknowledged receipt of payment on EDAS. Please allow for postal delays by registering and making a payment well in advance of the final manuscript submission deadline.

Please note that we do not entertain cancellation of registrations, and no refunds will be provided.

Accommodation

Use this form to request accommodation on campus after you have registered.

SPCOM 2018 accommodation information page

Please note that very limited accommodation is available. Accomodation is not guaranteed, and is given on a first-come-first-served basis (based on the date of the last update with arrival and departure information). If you requested accommodation, but later decide that you don't need it, then please remember to cancel your request.    

Payment should be made at the reception desk of the guest house at the time of departure.
