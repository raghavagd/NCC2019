TECHNICAL CO-SPONSORS

PLENARY SPEAKERS

1.  The nominal duration of each plenary presentation is 55 minutes (including speaker introduction). Following this, time permitting, 5 minutes are reserved for questions and answers. Discussion beyond these 5 minutes will have to take place offline during the coffee breaks.

2.  Session chairs will enforce these time constraints.

3.  A laptop & projector will be provided for the presentations.

4.  You are kindly requested to upload your slides to the local computer connected to the projector at least 10 minutes *BEFORE* the beginning of the session so as to avoid unnecessary technical problems and delays.

5.  A student volunteer will be available in the JN Tata Auditorium to assist you.  

INVITED SPEAKERS

1.  The duration of each invited talk is 26 minutes, including time for questions and answers.

2.  Session chairs will enforce these time constraints.

3.  A laptop & projector will be provided for the presentations.

4.  You are kindly requested to upload your slides to the local computer connected to the projector at least 10 minutes *BEFORE* the beginning of the session so as to avoid unnecessary technical problems and delays.

5.  A student volunteer will be available in your session hall to assist you.  

CONTRIBUTED TALKS

1.  The duration of each contributed talk is 18 minutes, including time for questions and answers.

2.  Session chairs will enforce these time constraints.

3.  A laptop & projector will be provided for the presentations.

4.  You are kindly requested to upload your slides to the local computer connected to the projector at least 10 minutes *BEFORE* the beginning of the session so as to avoid unnecessary technical problems and delays.

5.  A student volunteer will be available in your session hall to assist you.
